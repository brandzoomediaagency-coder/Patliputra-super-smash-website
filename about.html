Our Journey & Purpose

Patliputra Super Smash (PSS) was established with a singular mission: to provide a professional, transparent, and merit-based platform for the immense cricketing talent hidden in every corner of Bihar. Our journey began with the realization that thousands of gifted athletes lack the right infrastructure to showcase their potential to national scouts.

Over the years, PSS has grown into a prestigious T20 league that combines world-class facilities with a rigorous selection process. We are not just a tournament; we are a talent incubator dedicated to bridging the gap between local street cricket and professional state-level glory.

Mission

To identify Bihar's finest cricketers through transparent trials and nurture them under the guidance of expert mentors for professional success.

Vision

To become the premier feeder league for national cricket, ensuring that no talent in Bihar goes unnoticed due to lack of opportunity.
