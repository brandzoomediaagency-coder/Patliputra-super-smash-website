About Patliputra Super Smash

Empowering Bihar's Cricketing Dreams

Our Story

Patliputra Super Smash (PSS) was founded with a singular vision: to bridge the gap between street cricket and professional stadiums for the talented youth of Bihar. We recognized that while the passion for cricket is immense, the opportunities for structured development and professional exposure were limited.

Since our inception, we have been committed to providing a transparent platform where merit is the only currency. Our league brings together the best coaches, professional match standards, and an environment that fosters growth and sportsmanship.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To be the premier scouting and development platform in Eastern India, producing world-class cricketers who represent the nation.

Our Mission

To provide professional training, competitive match-play, and holistic development to grassroots talent through fair trials and expert coaching.

Our Commitment

We are dedicated to maintaining the highest standards of integrity in player selection. Our focus remains on grassroots development, ensuring that even players from the most remote areas of Bihar have a chance to shine under the global spotlight.
