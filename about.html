ABOUT THE LEAGUE

The professional road to cricket excellence in Bihar.

OUR MISSION

Patliputra Super Smash (PSS) is Bihar's premier T20 cricket league, dedicated to uncovering and nurturing the vast untapped cricketing talent in the state. Inspired by professional global league formats, PSS provides a high-stakes, professional environment for local players to showcase their skills.

Our goal is to act as a bridge between local "gully" cricket and the professional national spotlight. We combine rigorous selection trials with elite coaching and franchise-based tournament play to ensure that every talented player gets a fair chance to succeed.

Vision

To be the leading talent incubator for cricket in East India, producing players who represent the nation.

Commitment

100% transparent selection process overseen by impartial experts and BCCI-certified coaches.

Exposure

Providing digital tracking and media coverage to help players connect with professional scouts.
