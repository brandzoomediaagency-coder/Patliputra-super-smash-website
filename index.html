Who We Are

Patliputra Super Smash is more than just a cricket league; it's a launchpad for the next generation of cricketing legends. Based in the heart of Bihar, we are dedicated to identifying, nurturing, and promoting grassroots talent.

Our mission is to provide a fair, transparent, and professional platform where every aspiring cricketer gets a chance to showcase their skills under the spotlight of a premium league format.
