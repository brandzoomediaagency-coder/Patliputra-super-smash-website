Selection Process

Your Path to Professional Cricket Starts Here

How It Works

1

Online Registration

Fill out the detailed registration form with your age, category, and playing role.

2

Trial Invitation

Shortlisted candidates will receive trial details and IDs via WhatsApp.

3

Ground Trials

Participate in net sessions and practice matches under the observation of our selection committee.

4

Final Selection

Top performers will be drafted into teams for the Patliputra Super Smash League.

Evaluation Criteria

  • Technique: Solid basics for batsmen and proper action for bowlers.
  • Fitness: Agility, endurance, and speed on the field.
  • Attitude: Sportsmanship, coachability, and team player mindset.
  • Performance: Impact in match scenarios during trials.
