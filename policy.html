Privacy & Policy

Your data security and privacy protocols

Data Collection & Use

Welcome to Patliputra Super Smash. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

1. Information We Collect

When you register for our trials, we collect personal details including your full name, age, phone number, city, and player category. Additionally, we collect identification documents and photographs as part of our mandatory documentation process.

2. Use of Information

The information collected is used solely for the purpose of trial organization, player identification, and league communications. This includes updates regarding trial dates, selection results, and league news via phone, SMS, or WhatsApp.

3. Consent for Contact

By submitting your information through our registration or contact forms, you provide explicit consent to be contacted by the Patliputra Super Smash team for marketing, promotional updates, and operational information.

4. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to ensure that your identification documents and personal data are stored securely and are not accessible to unauthorized third parties.
