Frequently Asked Questions

Find Quick Answers to Common Queries

How can I register for the trials?
You can register directly by clicking the "Register Now" button in the menu and filling out the online form. You will receive a confirmation message once submitted.
What is the age eligibility for different categories?
Junior Category: Under 15 years. Senior Category: 16 to 23 years. Age is calculated based on government-issued birth certificates as of January 1st of the current year.
Where are the trials conducted?
Trials are conducted across multiple venues in Patna and surrounding districts. Exact venue details will be sent to your registered WhatsApp number 48 hours before the trial date.
Is there a registration fee?
Yes, there is a nominal processing fee for ground trials. Payment can be made online through UPI or Credit/Debit cards on our secure payment gateway after completing the registration form.
How will the selection results be announced?
Shortlisted players will be contacted individually via WhatsApp and the final list will also be published on our social media handles and the official website.
