Injury & Safety Rules

Your Safety is Our Top Priority

Mandatory Warm-up

All players must participate in the pre-match warm-up sessions led by our trainers to minimize the risk of muscle injuries.

Safety Equipment

Helmet, pads, gloves, and abdominal guards are mandatory during trials and matches. No player will be allowed on the field without standard safety gear.

Medical Support

On-site physiotherapy and first-aid support are available at all times during matches and training sessions.

Code of Conduct

Strict anti-doping policy and zero tolerance for unsportsmanlike behavior or physical aggression on the field.

Emergency Protocol

  • Immediate cessation of play in case of head injury.
  • Ambulance on standby for major traumas.
  • Contacting next of kin as per the player registration database.
